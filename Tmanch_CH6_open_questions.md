# Chapter 6 Open Questions and Records Plan

## Purpose

This file converts the Chapter 6 network leads into records programs. Each program identifies:

- the question;
- likely custodian;
- date range;
- names and search terms;
- records requested;
- proof threshold; and
- the conclusion that would change if the records are found.

The period is January 2021 through January 19, 2025, with later records requested when they concern the disposition of an event inside the period.

---

# Priority 1 — TMTG emergency financing and public-equity ownership

## Program 1: Federal disposition of the Paxum / ES Family Trust inquiry

### Question

What did SDNY, FBI, HSI, FinCEN, or the SEC determine concerning the reported $8 million in TMTG emergency financing?

### Custodians

- U.S. Attorney's Office for the Southern District of New York / EOUSA
- FBI New York Field Office
- Homeland Security Investigations, El Dorado Task Force
- FinCEN
- SEC Division of Enforcement
- IRS Criminal Investigation, if referred

### Date range

October 1, 2021 through December 31, 2025.

### Search terms

- Trump Media & Technology Group
- TMTG
- Truth Social
- Digital World Acquisition Corporation / DWAC
- ES Family Trust
- Paxum Bank
- Anton Postolnikov
- Angel Pacheco
- Patrick Orlando
- Michael Shvartsman
- Gerald Shvartsman
- Bruce Garelick
- Will Wilkerson
- Phillip Juhan
- Devin Nunes
- Donald Trump Jr.
- $2 million / $6 million / $8 million
- Dominica

### Records requested

1. Case-opening and closing records.
2. Final declination, prosecution, or referral memoranda.
3. Search-warrant applications and returns that are no longer sealed.
4. Subpoenas, summonses, and document-return indexes.
5. Bank, trust, wire, beneficial-owner, and source-of-funds analyses.
6. Suspicious Activity Reports only to the extent legally releasable; otherwise aggregate or referral metadata.
7. Interagency referral and consultation records.
8. Existing records identifying case numbers, file classifications, and related investigations.

### Proof threshold

- **Strengthens Russian-source hypothesis:** traced funds from a Russian state, state-linked company, sanctioned person, intelligence-linked individual, or concealed beneficial owner.
- **Weakens hypothesis:** verified private lawful funds with no Russian-state or criminal provenance.
- **Knowledge threshold:** documents showing TMTG directors, Trump, or counsel were informed of the actual source.

### Federal request module

```text
Pursuant to FOIA, I request final, releasable records concerning the
investigation, review, referral, or disposition of two reported loans
totaling approximately $8 million received by Trump Media & Technology
Group in or about December 2021 and February 2022 through Paxum Bank
and/or ES Family Trust.

Please search records concerning TMTG, Truth Social, DWAC, ES Family
Trust, Paxum Bank, Anton Postolnikov, Angel Pacheco, Patrick Orlando,
Michael Shvartsman, Gerald Shvartsman, and Will Wilkerson for the period
October 1, 2021 through December 31, 2025.

I request case-opening and closing records, final declination or referral
memoranda, releasable search-warrant and subpoena indexes, financial
tracing summaries, beneficial-ownership analyses, interagency
communications, and existing records identifying related case numbers.
I do not request grand-jury material or protected SAR contents. Please
release all reasonably segregable portions.
```
