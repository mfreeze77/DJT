# Chapter 2 Primary-Source and Documentary Record Inventory

## Purpose

This inventory identifies the records supporting the expanded Chapter 2. “Primary source” is used narrowly for contemporaneous recordings, court opinions, government enforcement records, archival intelligence files, participant writings, and original documents. Investigative reporting and retrospective witness accounts are separately labeled.

Each entry states what the record supports and what it does not establish.
