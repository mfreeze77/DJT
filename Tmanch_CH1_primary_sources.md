# Chapter 1 Primary and Documentary Source Inventory

This inventory distinguishes records that directly document an event from later reporting that describes archival material not yet reproduced in the repository.

## Source classification

- **P1 — Primary public record:** law, official archive catalog, official sports record, FBI release, court filing, or original government record.
- **P2 — Archive-based documentary reporting:** reporting based on review of original records and interviews with custodians or participants.
- **P3 — Contemporaneous reporting:** published near the event and useful for how identity or claims were presented at the time.
- **S — Secondary context:** retrospective history or biography.
